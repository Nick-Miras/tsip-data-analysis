Site Title: Mollah Metal (Lead breaking & smelting) Works , Sadhapur, Bongaon, Savar, Dhaka
Site Description: This is a local ULAB breaking and smelting site situated at Bilbagil, Sadhapur, Bongaon union of Savar upazila, Dhaka district.   They break used batteries and sort out different parts of the batteries for recycling. They smelt lead at this site in an open place.   This area is a sparsely populated alluvial agricultural plain land. There is a river close to the site.  Lead and arsenic is the main pollutant of this area which are polluting the air,soil and water of nearby areas. People are exposed to lead and arsenic toxicity through dermal contact and inhalation/ingestion.
